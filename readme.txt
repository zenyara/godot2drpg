=======================================================
* Mechanics

  * Auction - website to buy/sell with other players

  * Audio - adjust audio/volume

  * Banking - store valuables and keep inventory clear

  * Console - commands console `

  * Energy - meter for melee chars (wars in WoW have Rage, fills during combat)

  * Experience - meter

  * Health - meter

  * Inventory - bag slots with items inside

  * Mana - meter

  * Map - view the map (likely the overwold)

  * Mini-map - on-screen mini-map (what's nearby, 6 screens)

  * Paper doll - character equipment panel

  * Perks shop - $$$ perk items/cosmetics, pets, companions, bag slots, Turtle-WoW-like

  * Pets - control them to help fight for you

  * Pet Inventory - armor/equip your pet

  * Portal - send player to another area
 
  * Quit - exit the game (button)

  * Rebirth - start at 0 (when available) to gain permanent stats/reward/rank

  * Re-spec - $$ players can reset their skill tree

  * Swimming - some areas are swim necessary

  * Transfers - between characters (unlock $$)

  * Vending - sell to vendors and buy needed items 


======================================================
* Commands (Console)

  * Die - kill your player character
  * Exit - quit game
  * Fly - move around freely
  * Give - opens items panel to give target whatever you click on from it.
  * God - be invincible
  * Idea or Note - add a note in the list
  * Kill - kill targeted player or NPC
  * Killall - kill all nearby players and NPCs (Not self)
  * Spawn - opens spawner panel with creatures and items (tabbed) to spawn-in
  * Teleport or Warp - opens a map to click on where to instantly teleport to
